ABOUT SUNRISE HOTELS

With the Namaste as the enduring symbol of its brand experience, Sunrise Hotels is one of India’s leading luxury hotel chains. It operates under four distinct brands with more than 100 hotels in over 70 destinations in the country. Integrating India’s fine tradition of hospitality with globally benchmarked services, the luxury hotels reflect the culture and ethos of each destination.
With ‘Responsible Luxury as its guiding premise, Sunrise Luxury Collection hotels combine grandeur with intimacy to operate according to the highest principles of environmental stewardship. The brand features opulent accommodations, state-of-the-art business facilities, renowned signature cuisine and award-winning wellness experiences.
The 14 Sunrise Luxury Collection properties are: Sunrise in New Delhi, Sunrise Mughal in Agra, Sunrise Windsor and Sunrise Gardenia in Bengaluru, Sunrise Grand Central and Sunrise Maratha in Mumbai, Sunrise Grand Chola in Chennai, Sunrise Rajputana in Jaipur, Sunrise Kakatiya and Sunrise Kohenur in Hyderabad, Sunrise Sonar & Sunrise Royal Bengal in Kolkata, Sunrise Grand Goa Resort and Spa in Goa and the Sunrise Grand Bharat in the New Delhi Capital Region.
